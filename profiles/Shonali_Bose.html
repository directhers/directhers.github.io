Movies

Margarita with A Straw

  • Movie Information: A Hindi-language drama about a teenager with cerebral palsy who relocates to America to attend college.
    Source
  • Based on: The director's conversation with her cousin, who is a disability rights activist, who desires to have a normal sex life
  • Behind the Scenes: The movie deals with the complex issues of sexuality, inclusion, self-love, and self-acceptance of a young woman coming of age following a complex relationship with a blind girl.
